## **The Clash of Faith - Full Battle Account**

_"Even gods cannot deny the power of mortal ambition."_  
— High Commander Aelindra Starweaver

### **Initial Forces:**

#### **Alliance Forces:**
- **Sunfield Hold (Humans):** 8,000 troops
  - Disciplined infantry
  - Cavalry scouts
  - Field engineers
- **Lorianthil (Elves):** 3,000 troops
  - Forest rangers
  - Elite archers
  - Spellblades
- **Grimhaldir (Dwarves):** 2,000 troops
  - Heavy infantry
  - Siege crews
  - Mining experts

#### **Orcish Forces (Competing Tribes):** 12,000 warriors
- Ironclaw Tribe (largest)
- Bloodfang Warband
- Blacktooth Raiders

#### **Troll Armies (Forest Claimants):** 4,000 warriors
- Shadowclad Clan
- Beast tamers
- Tribal shamans

#### **Goblin Forces (Experimental Clans):** 6,000 troops
- Veilfire researchers
- Demolition teams
- Scrap engineers

### **Day-by-Day Breakdown:**

**Day 1 - The Discovery**  
_"One vein of crystals. Six armies. No chance for peace."_

- Dawn: Dwarven miners uncover major Veilfire deposit
- Midday: Word spreads as scouts report troop movements
- Evening: First clashes between advance forces
- Night: Goblin sappers begin probing defenses

Multiple orc warbands arrive separately, already eyeing each other with suspicion.

---

**Day 2 - Opening Moves**  
_"The orcs fought each other as much as us. The trolls were smarter - they waited."_

- Morning: Orcs launch uncoordinated attacks
- Midday: Alliance forms defensive line
- Afternoon: Goblin experiments cause chaos
- Evening: Troll forces probe the forest edges

The Ironclaw and Bloodfang chiefs kill each other in a personal duel, creating command vacuum.

---

**Day 3 - Divine Interest**  
_"When Selûne's light touched the field, we knew the battle had changed."_

- Dawn: Selûne's Avatar appears, drawn by crystal energy
- Midday: Limited divine intervention begins
- Afternoon: Crystal energies spike unpredictably
- Night: Both sides regroup and fortify

Trolls use the confusion to establish first footholds in forest regions.

---

**Day 4 - Mortal Struggles**  
_"Gods might watch, but men still bleed."_

- Morning: Alliance launches coordinated counterattack
- Midday: Orcish forces begin consolidating under surviving leaders
- Afternoon: Goblin experiments yield results
- Evening: Trolls expand their gains

Selûne's avatar focuses on protection rather than combat.

---

**Day 5 - Crystal Chaos**  
_"The Veilfire changed everything it touched."_

- Morning: Crystal deposits become unstable
- Midday: Multiple detonations rock the battlefield
- Afternoon: Forces withdraw from crystal zones
- Evening: Arkim's transformation during evacuation

The battlefield splits into safer zones away from crystal deposits.

---

**Day 6 - Strategic Withdrawal**  
_"Victory became survival. Survival became enough."_

- Dawn: Alliance forces begin planned withdrawal
- Midday: Orcs retrieve their dead chiefs
- Afternoon: Trolls secure forest positions
- Evening: Goblins collect samples and data

Divine presence fades as mortals settle their own fates.

---

**Day 7 - Aftermath**  
_"No victor. Only survivors."_

- Dawn: Final skirmishes
- Midday: Forces withdraw to safe distances
- Afternoon: Crystal zones declared hazardous
- Night: Plains left scarred but not uninhabitable

### **True Outcomes:**

**Alliance:**
- Secured western approaches to Sunfield Hold
- Prevented total enemy victory
- Maintained unity between races

**Orcs:**
- Lost several major chiefs
- Began process of limited unification
- Gained respect for Alliance strength

**Trolls:**
- Secured forest footholds
- Established Shadowclad Clan dominance
- Gained defensible territory

**Goblins:**
- Gathered valuable research data
- Tested new Veilfire applications
- Advanced their understanding

### **Lasting Impact:**

- Creation of buffer zones between powers
- Establishment of new territorial boundaries
- Beginning of orcish consolidation
- Foundation of troll forest holdings
- Advancement of goblin Veilfire research
- Rare but significant divine intervention
- Arkim's transformation as symbol of hope

The Clash of Faith proved that even with divine interest, mortal ambitions and actions shape the world's future. Each faction emerged changed, leading to the current balance of power across the region.
