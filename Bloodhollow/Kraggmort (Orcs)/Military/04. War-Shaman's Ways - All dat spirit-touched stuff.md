# War-Shaman's Ways

*"Da spirits don't care how strong yer arm is. They care how strong yer blood runs, how deep yer scars go, and how many ancestors ya got screamin' in yer head."*  
— Grimtusk da Bonechanter, Head War-Shaman of da Ironclaw

## What Makes a War-Shaman

Ain't just about swingin' an axe or leadin' a charge. War-Shamans gotta talk ta spirits, read da bones, and keep da old ways strong. Every war needs both muscle and magic - dat's what makes us different from dem stupid beasts we fight.
