# Blood-Rank Trials

*"Ya want respect? Ya gotta bleed for it. Ya want power? Ya gotta take it. Ya want glory? Den ya gotta earn it wit every bone ya break - includin' yer own."*  
— Warlord Thokk Ironhide

## What Makes a Leader

Every orc's got da same chance to rise. Don't matter if yer da runty whelp or da chief's own blood - strength speaks louder dan birth. These trials mark da path from warrior to warlord, each step paid in blood and broken bones.

## Blood-Ranks

### Blood-Crowned (Warlord)
*"Da one dat stands when everyone else has fallen."*

**What Ya Need:**
- Won three major wars or crushed five rival chiefs
- Killed somethin' dat shoulda killed you
- Got a warband dat follows yer banner
- Made da shamans nod in respect
- Got da old Warlord's skull on yer belt

**Ways Ta Prove It:**
- Lead a war dat burns kingdoms
- Crush a rival clan's whole leadership
- Kill somethin' legendary (dragons count extra)
- Unite warring tribes under yer banner

**What Ya Get:**
- Final say in all things
- First pick of all loot
- Command of all warbands
- Right to wear da Warlord's marks

### Blood-Honored (War-Chief)
*"Da one dat leads da charge and lives."*

**What Ya Need:**
- Led two big raids or won three clan fights
- Got scars dat tell good stories
- Command respect from beast-masters
- Got warriors eager to follow

**Ways Ta Prove It:**
- Hold yer ground against bigger forces
- Lead successful raids on hard targets
- Keep yer warband strong and loyal
- Win glory in clan battles

**What Ya Get:**
- Command of major raids
- Choice war gear
- Strong position in clan
- Right to challenge higher ranks

### Blood-Proven (War-Captain)
*"Da one dat others follow into battle."*

**What Ya Need:**
- First blood in real battle
- Led small raids dat worked
- Got respect from warriors
- Know how ta plan a fight

**Ways Ta Prove It:**
- Lead successful hunting parties
- Win personal combat challenges
- Raid valuable targets
- Show ya can think in battle

**What Ya Get:**
- Lead hunting parties
- Better weapons and armor
- Right to plan small raids
- Train new warriors

## How Ya Rise Up

### Challenge Rules
1. **Da Challenge**
   - Any warrior can challenge their direct boss
   - Gotta be witnessed by da clan
   - Fight's to da death or surrender
   - Winner takes position

2. **Da Trial**
   - Show yer strength
   - Prove yer cunning
   - Demonstrate leadership
   - Survive da pain

3. **Da Recognition**
   - War-Shaman's blessing
   - Clan's acceptance
   - Blood marking ceremony
   - Position claiming

### What Counts As Proof

**Big Victories:**
- Da Siege of Stonehaven
- Da Battle of Ashgate
- Da Blackroot Siege
- Any clan-joining battle

**Worthy Fights:**
- Defending territory against bigger forces
- Leading raids on fortified places
- Special missions dat need brains too
- Big beast hunting

**Extra Glory For:**
- Fighting dragons
- Killing champions
- Taking strongholds
- Uniting rival clans

## What Different Ranks Do

### Blood-Crowned (Warlord)
- Command all war efforts
- Choose clan territories
- Deal with other races
- Lead biggest battles

### Blood-Honored (War-Chief)
- Lead clan in war
- Control clan territory
- Organize big raids
- Train war leaders

### Blood-Proven (War-Captain)
- Lead war parties
- Plan raid tactics
- Train warriors
- Hold territory

## Special Rules

### Beast-Master Things
