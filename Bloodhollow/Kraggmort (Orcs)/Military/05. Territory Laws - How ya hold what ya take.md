# Territory Laws

*"Ya only own what ya can hold. An' ya only hold what ya can kill to keep."*  
— Warlord Thokk Ironhide, markin' da borders of Kraggmort

## What Makes Territory Yours

Simple truth - land belongs to whoever's strong enough to keep it. Don't matter what fancy papers say or what other races think. Ya hold it, ya keep others out, ya make it worth somethin' - den it's yours until someone stronger takes it.
