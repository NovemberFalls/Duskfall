## Orc Leadership

**Warchief Groknak Skullcrusher**
- City: ScorchRidge
- Title: Supreme Leader of the Bloodfang Orcs
- Description: A massive orc with bulging muscles, covered in battle scars and war paint. Known for his brutality in battle and overwhelming force tactics.

**Warlord Thokk Ironhide**
- City: Bloodtide Bay
- Title: Supreme Leader of the Ironclaw Orcs
- Description: A towering orc with blue-tinted skin and ritual scarification across his body. Unified the Ironclaw clan and drives for orcish dominance over the seas.
- Death: [[0099-09-01 - Emberfall - The Blackroot Siege]]

**Trade Baron Nazgrel Goldfang**
- City: Reaver's Forge
- Title: Master of Ironclaw Trade and Diplomacy
- Description: A well-groomed orc with gold-capped tusks and fingers adorned with stolen rings. Built the Ironclaw's trade empire through business acumen and brutal intimidation.

**Shaman-King Vroth the Ashen**

- **City:** Gor'Zhaka
- **Title:** Chieftain of the Bonegrinder Clan
- **Description:** Draped in robes of tanned hide and bones, Vroth’s presence commands both fear and reverence. His body is adorned with arcane markings, and his eyes burn with the flames of forgotten spirits. Vroth believes the orcs’ salvation lies in blood rituals and communing with the spirits of the land, forging dark pacts that empower his warriors with forbidden magics.

**Warmaster Kharzul the Red**
- **City:** ScorchRidge
- **Title:** War Chief of the Bloodfang Vanguard
- **Description:** Kharzul’s skin is dark crimson, his armor fused to his flesh from countless battles. His jagged axe, **“Bloodreaver,”** is said to drip with the souls of his enemies. He carries himself with grim authority, often seen leading from the front lines, carving through foes with reckless abandon.
- **Backstory:** Kharzul rose to prominence by challenging and defeating three rival war chiefs in a single night. His relentless ferocity has made him Groknak’s most trusted war leader, and he oversees the Bloodfang’s vanguard forces, ensuring no enemy breaches their mountain stronghold.

**Sea Reaver Jorza Bonehook**
- **City:** Bloodtide Bay
- **Title:** Pirate-Lord of the Ironclaw Raiders
- **Description:** Jorza’s skin is marked by deep sea scars, and his left hand has been replaced by a jagged bone hook carved from the jaw of a sea leviathan. His braided black hair is adorned with shark teeth, and he wears a patchwork coat taken from the fallen captains he’s slain.
- **Backstory:** Jorza commands the deadliest ships of the Ironclaw raiders, striking fear into coastal towns and rival fleets. His raids have crippled trade routes across Bloodhollow’s seas, and his growing fleet threatens to rival even Thokk Ironhide’s navy.

**Forge Warden Dregnok Ironjaw**
- **City:** Reaver's Forge
- **Title:** Master Smith of the Ashmaw Clan
- **Description:** Dregnok’s lower jaw is metal, the result of a forging accident during his youth. His massive hammer, **“Moltenheart,”** glows faintly with trapped embers. Dregnok is broader than most orcs, his skin coated in soot and burn scars, and his voice carries the deep rumble of a forge.
- **Backstory:** Dregnok oversees the forges of Reaver’s Forge, crafting war machines and arms for the Ashmaw. Known for his brutal discipline, he smelts the bones of his enemies into the very weapons his warriors wield, ensuring nothing goes to waste.

**Chieftain Graska the Wretched**
- **City:** Gor'Zhaka
- **Title:** Bonegrinder Clan Warlord
- **Description:** Graska’s twisted frame is covered in bone armor, and his face is obscured by a horned mask carved from the skull of a great beast. His spiked staff rattles with enchanted bones, and he moves with an unnatural, jerking gait.
- **Backstory:** A loyal follower of Shaman-King Vroth, Graska enforces his will among the Bonegrinder Clan. His methods are savage, often employing dark curses and necrotic magic to crush dissent. Rumors persist that Graska’s body is sustained by the spirits he has bound to his will.

**Bladewarden Nargul Dreadfist**
- **City:** ScorchRidge
- **Title:** Champion of the Bloodfang Arena
- **Description:** Nargul is a towering orc whose left arm has been replaced with a jagged iron gauntlet, crudely fused to the flesh. His tusks have been sharpened into blades, and he wears the skulls of past challengers as pauldrons.
- **Backstory:** Nargul rose through the brutal arenas of ScorchRidge, besting dozens of orcs in combat to claim the title of Champion. He now trains Groknak’s elite warriors, ensuring that only the strongest rise to serve the Bloodfang.

**Stormcaller Rokgra the Howling**
- **City:** Bloodtide Bay
- **Title:** Shaman of the Ironclaw Tempests
- **Description:** Rokgra’s skin is gray-blue, crackling with latent storm energy. Her hair floats as if caught in an eternal breeze, and her staff is crowned with jagged coral and stormglass. Thunder follows her wherever she walks.
- **Backstory:** Rokgra communes with the raging seas and tempests that lash Bloodtide Bay, calling forth lightning and storms to shatter enemy fleets. Some say she was born during a sea storm and carries the wrath of the elements within her veins.
