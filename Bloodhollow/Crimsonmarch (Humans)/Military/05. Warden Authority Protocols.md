# Warden Authority Protocols

*"A Warden's domain is their life, and their life belongs to their domain."*  
— Warden Alric Stoneveil

## Overview
Wardens are Level 3 Regional Commanders who hold absolute authority over critical installations, fortifications, and districts within Emberfall's sphere of influence. Unlike mobile force commanders, their power is tied to specific domains, where they exercise near-autonomous control.

## Domain Authority

### Absolute Powers Within Domain
1. **Military Command**
   - Full control of garrison forces
   - Defense strategy implementation
   - Resource allocation
   - Training oversight

2. **Civil Authority**
   - Population management
   - Resource distribution
   - Justice administration
   - Infrastructure maintenance

3. **Security Powers**
   - Access control
   - Threat response
   - Investigation initiation
   - Emergency powers

4. **Resource Control**
   - Supply management
   - Personnel assignment
   - Equipment allocation
   - Facility maintenance

## Command Relationships

### Superior Officers
1. **Lord Commander**

2. **General**
   - Operational coordination

### Visiting Forces
1. **Higher Ranks**
   - Must respect domain authority
   - Coordinate operations

2. **Equal Ranks**
   - Full cooperation required

3. **Lower Ranks**
   - Complete domain submission
   - Follow local protocols
   - Report to domain chain
   - Support domain operations
