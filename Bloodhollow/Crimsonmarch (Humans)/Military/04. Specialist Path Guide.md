*"Faith and flame are the twin blades of our purpose."*  
— High Inquisitor Varian Marrow

## Inquisitorial Path

### Level 2 - High Command
**High Inquisitor**
- Example: High Inquisitor Varian Marrow
- Commands: All Inquisitorial Forces
- Primary Units: Cathedral Militant, Chainhold Guards
- Core Powers:
  - Divine judgment
  - Religious authority
  - Heresy declarations
  - Final sentencing
- Key Responsibilities:
  - Religious doctrine
  - Inquisitorial policy
  - Major investigations
  - Heretic hunting

### Level 3 - Regional Command
**Senior Inquisitor**
- Commands: Regional Inquisitorial Forces
- Primary Units: Investigation Teams
- Core Powers:
  - Major blessings
  - Interrogation rites
  - Field judgments
  - Combat prayers
- Key Responsibilities:
  - Regional investigations
  - Heretic suppression
  - Evidence gathering
  - Team coordination

### Level 4 - Unit Command
**Inquisitor**
- Commands: Investigation Unit
- Primary Units: Inquisitorial Squads
- Core Powers:
  - Minor blessings
  - Truth compulsion
  - Sacred flames
  - Protective wards
- Key Responsibilities:
  - Direct investigation
  - Local enforcement
  - Evidence collection
  - Field operations

### Level 5 - Support Command
**Inquisitor Adept**
- Commands: Small Teams
- Primary Units: Support Personnel
- Core Powers:
  - Basic rites
  - Detection prayers
  - Purifying flames
  - Protection prayers
- Key Responsibilities:
  - Assist investigations
  - Basic interrogations
  - Evidence handling
  - Support operations
