# Emberfall Legion Combat Status System

*"Rank commands respect. Combat Status commands awe."*  
— General Darius Crownhold

## Overview
The Combat Status system exists parallel to formal ranks, recognizing exceptional combat prowess and battlefield experience. These honors can be earned by any member of the Legion regardless of their path or rank, serving as marks of martial distinction.

## Status Levels

### Veteran Champion
*"Those who have stared into the abyss and made it blink first."*

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 3 major campaigns or 5 significant battles
- Personal victory against superior enemy force
- Demonstrated leadership under extreme duress
- Survival of at least one "extinction-level" combat event
- Nomination by existing Veteran Champion
- Unanimous approval by Champion's Council

**Notable Achievements That Qualify:**
- Successfully defending a position against 3:1 or greater odds
- Leading a successful operation when all superior officers have fallen
- Surviving and completing mission after majority of unit is lost
- Killing an enemy champion or commander in single combat

**Privileges:**
- Access to Champion's Council meetings
- Authority to nominate new Champions
- Priority access to Legion arsenals
- Right to bear personal heraldry

### Ironborn Veteran
*"The hammer that has struck a thousand times and never broken."*

**Requirements:**
- Participation in 2 major campaigns or 3 significant battles
- Survival of at least one major siege
- Demonstrated tactical acumen
- Recommendation from unit commander
- Approval by 3 Veteran Champions

**Notable Achievements That Qualify:**
- Holding position against 2:1 odds
- Successful completion of high-risk missions
- Multiple confirmed kills of significant enemies
- Saving unit members through tactical decisions

**Privileges:**
- Access to veteran equipment stores
- Authority to lead small unit tactical operations
- Right to train initiates
- Ceremonial armor distinctions

### Ironborn Warrior
*"The first crucible of combat mastery."*

**Requirements:**
- Completion of first major campaign or 2 battles
- Survival of combat engagement
- Basic tactical competence demonstrated
- Recommendation from immediate superior
- Approval by 1 Veteran Champion

**Notable Achievements That Qualify:**
- First confirmed kill in combat
- Successful defense of assigned position
- Completion of combat mission objectives
- Demonstrated courage under fire

**Privileges:**
- Access to advanced training sessions
- Right to wear Ironborn insignia
- Eligibility for veteran missions
- Basic ceremonial distinctions

## Earning Combat Status

### Application Process
1. **Nomination Phase**
   - Submission of combat record
   - Letters of recommendation
   - Witness testimonials
   - Battle documentation

2. **Trial Phase**
   - Combat proficiency tests
   - Tactical scenario evaluations
   - Leadership assessments (if applicable)
   - Psychological evaluation

3. **Review Phase**
   - Evaluation by Champion's Council
   - Verification of achievements
   - Character assessment
   - Final deliberation

### Qualifying Combat Events

**Major Campaigns:**
- The Siege of Stonehaven
- The Battle of Ashgate Approach
- The Blackroot Siege
- The Black Anvil Revolt

**Significant Battles:**
- Defense actions involving 100+ combatants
- Siege operations lasting 3+ days
- Special operations against priority targets
- Major urban combat operations

**Special Circumstances:**
- Veilfire containment operations
- Demon incursion responses
- Dragon encounters
- Undead horde engagements

## Status Effects by Path

### Military Command
- Enhanced authority in tactical situations
- Priority in combat command decisions
- Additional weight in strategy councils
- Access to elite units

### Inquisitorial Path
- Authority in combat against heretics
- Priority in militant operations
- Enhanced status in military coordination
- Access to combat-oriented relics

### War-Mage Path
- Priority in combat magic deployment
- Enhanced authority in magical warfare
- Access to combat spell repositories
- Tactical priority in combined operations

## Status Recognition

### Visual Indicators
- **Veteran Champion:** Gold skull on black field
- **Ironborn Veteran:** Silver skull on red field
- **Ironborn Warrior:** Bronze skull on crimson field

### Ceremonial Privileges
- Right to bear status symbols
- Participation in honor guards
- Speaking rights at military councils
- Precedence in military ceremonies

### Equipment Rights
- Access to enhanced equipment
- Right to modify standard kit
- Priority in requisitions
- Access to special weapons

## Status Revocation

### Grounds for Removal
- Cowardice in battle
- Tactical incompetence leading to casualties
- Insubordination in combat
- Failure to maintain combat readiness

### Review Process
1. Formal complaint filed
2. Champion's Council review
3. Combat record evaluation
4. Final judgment by Lord Commander

### Appeals
- One appeal permitted
- Must present new evidence
- Requires champion sponsor
- Final decision by Council of War

## Integration with Regular Forces

### Command Scenarios
1. **Equal Rank Conflicts**
   - Higher status takes tactical lead
   - Base rank maintains administrative authority
   - Status holder advises on combat matters

2. **Mixed Unit Operations**
   - Status holders lead combat elements
   - Regular ranks maintain unit cohesion
   - Collaborative tactical planning

3. **Emergency Situations**
   - Status authority increases
   - Can assume tactical control
   - Must defer to higher ranks when possible

## Training and Mentorship

### Champion's Duties
- Training new candidates
- Evaluating potential status holders
- Maintaining combat standards
- Preserving combat knowledge

### Knowledge Transfer
- Combat technique instruction
- Tactical wisdom sharing
- Leadership development
- Traditional combat lore

### Legacy Preservation
- Recording combat histories
- Documenting battle techniques
- Maintaining status traditions
- Preserving unit histories
