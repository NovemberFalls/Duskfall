## Human Lesser Leadership

**Warden Alric Stoneveil**
- **City**: Emberfall
- **Title:** Warden of the Sundered Gate  
- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral  
- **Race:** Human  
- **Age:** 52  
- **Appearance:** Alric is a towering man with broad shoulders and the pale complexion of someone who rarely sees the sun. His black iron plate is inscribed with wards of protection, and a long crimson cloak drapes over his shoulders. His square jaw is lined with silver stubble, and his eyes are as cold and unyielding as the gate he defends.  
- **Temperament:** Stoic, uncompromising, and deeply pragmatic. Alric values duty over personal gain, believing that the strength of the gate reflects the strength of Emberfall itself. His voice carries the weight of command, and few dare question his orders.  
- **History:** A veteran of the Veilfire War, Alric rose through the ranks by holding the gate during the [[0099-09-01 - Emberfall - The Blackroot Siege]], where he led a defense that lasted six days without relief. His commitment earned him the title of Warden, a position he has held ever since.  
- **Rumors:** It is whispered that Stoneveil never leaves the gate, and some say he sleeps standing, bound to the Sundered Gate by an ancient oath he made during the war. Others believe his heart was long replaced by a shard of black iron.  
- **Backstory**: A veteran of the Veilfire War, Alric rose through the ranks by holding the gate during the Blackroot Siege, where he led a defense that lasted six days without relief. His unwavering defense earned him the title "The Silent Bastion" and cemented his reputation as one of Emberfall's most steadfast defenders.

**General Darius Crownhold**
- **City**: Emberfall
- **Title:** Commander of the Black Bastion  
- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral  
- **Race:** Human  
- **Age:** 58  
- **Appearance:** Darius Crownhold is a tall, imposing figure with a muscular build earned through decades of war. His hair is streaked with silver, and his face is marked with old scars. He wears **blackened steel plate armor** adorned with crimson inlays, a symbol of his rank. A heavy greatsword, rumored to be enchanted by dwarves, rests across his back at all times.  
- **Temperament:** Crownhold is strict and unyielding. He demands excellence from his soldiers and punishes failure without mercy. Beneath his hardened exterior lies a deep respect for the sacrifices made by Emberfall’s warriors.  
- **History:** Crownhold earned his rank during the [[0099-09-01 - Emberfall - The Blackroot Siege]], commanding the vanguard that held the breach alongside Alric Stoneveil. His strategic brilliance turned the tide, saving the Iron Marches from collapse.  
- **Rumors:** Some say that Crownhold wields a **veiled blade**, forged during the Veilfire War. It is whispered that the blade drinks the souls of those it slays, binding them eternally to the Bastion’s defense.  
- **Backstory**: Crownhold earned his rank during the Blackroot Siege, commanding the vanguard that held the breach alongside Alric Stoneveil. His strategic brilliance turned the tide, saving the Iron Marches from collapse. He's known as "The Iron Bull" throughout Emberfall, his loyalty to the city unwavering.

**Morvyn "Redtooth" Blight**

- **Alignment:** Neutral Evil
- **Race:** Half-Orc

- **Rumors:** Some say Morvyn’s red-stained teeth are not from emberroot—but from biting the throats of those who challenged him in the pit. Others claim his gloves hide nails sharpened to talons.
- **Backstory**: Once a pit-fighter in the Iron Marches, known for pulling his opponents' teeth after every victory. When he grew too old to fight, he took over the Gauntlet, transforming it into a battleground of coin and cruelty.

**Tavik "The Quiet"**
- **City**: Emberfall
 - **Title:** Contract Dealer
- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
- **Race:** Half-Elf
- **Age:** 34
- **Appearance:** Tavik is pale, his sharp features framed by black hair that falls over one side of his face, obscuring a scar along his cheek. His left arm bears rows of tattooed contracts, inked and bound in old Elvish script. He moves silently, his boots making no sound against stone, and he rarely speaks louder than a whisper.

**High Inquisitor Varian Marrow**
- **City**: Emberfall
- **Title:** High Inquisitor of the Cathedral of Tyr’s Judgment
- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
- **Race:** Human
- **Age:** 56
- **Appearance:**  
    Varian is gaunt and severe, with long, graying hair tied back in tight braids. His face is marked by deep lines, and his cold, blue eyes seem to pierce the soul. He wears dark crimson robes adorned with silver chains, and his breastplate bears Tyr’s sigil—a set of scales bound by fire. His left hand is encased in black iron, said to have been burned by Veilfire during the **Battle of Blackroot Siege**.
- **Temperament:**  
    Uncompromising and merciless. Varian tolerates no deviation from the law and views leniency as weakness. To him, **justice is a blade, and compassion dulls its edge.**
- **History:**  
    Varian rose to prominence after leading the **Purge of Sunfield**, where an Asmodean cult was rooted out from the heart of the fortress. He personally interrogated and executed thirty cultists, an act that secured his position as High Inquisitor.
- **Rumors:**  
    Some say that Varian’s iron hand **screams at night**—echoes of the souls he has judged. Others claim that his heart was once pierced by an infernal blade and that he walks only through sheer will.
- **Backstory**: Rose to power during the Black Anvil Revolt, quelling insurgents by personally executing their leaders beneath the Veilfire Pit. His unwavering loyalty to Lord Commander Aldric Blackthorn earned him command over the Chainhold.
