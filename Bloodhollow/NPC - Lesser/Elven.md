## Elven Lesser Leadership

**Sister Elendra**
- **City**: Emberfall
- **Title:** Keeper of the Silent Chapel
- **Alignment:** Neutral
- **Race:** Elf
- **Age:** 144
- **Appearance:**  
    Elendra is slender and pale, her silver hair tied into braids. Her voice carries a melodic softness that calms even the most restless souls. Her robes are simple, adorned with faint crescent moon embroidery—symbolic of Sehanine, goddess of the moon and loss.
- **Temperament:**  
    Mysterious and withdrawn, Elendra rarely speaks outside of prayers. She believes deeply in the spiritual power of the Silent Chapel, often staying alone within its confines long after others have left.
- **Rumors:**  
    Some believe Elendra can commune with the dead, and that the whispers heard in the Silent Chapel are not imagined. It’s said she speaks to lost soldiers, guiding them to rest beneath the Veilfire Pyre.
