# Sacred Site Protocols

*"Every root remembers, every leaf watches, every branch guards. Nothing in our sacred places happens without purpose."*  
— Grove-Keeper Sylvaris Thornwhisper

## Overview
Sacred sites form the spiritual and tactical backbone of the Green Guard's defense network. Unlike human fortifications built to dominate the landscape, elven sacred sites exist in harmony with nature while serving as powerful defensive positions. Each site combines natural features, ancient magic, and living defenses into a seamless whole.

## Site Classifications

### Ancient Groves
*"Where the first trees remember the dawn of our people."*

**Characteristics:**
- Millennium-old silverleaf trees
- Natural magical convergence

**Security Levels:**
- Inner Grove (Elder Circle Only)
- Sacred Circle (Sentinel Circle and Above)
